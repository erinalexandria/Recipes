Honey-Pepper Coconut Shrimp

Although his breading technique at Rocky’s Hot Chicken Shack in Asheville, North Carolina is top secret, chef Rich Cundiff was kind enough to create this special riff on his coconut shrimp exclusively for us. A drizzle of syrupy honey glaze, infused with black pepper and lemon, adds a tart-sweet bite.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp finely grated lemon zest plus 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp black pepper, divided
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour (about 12 3/4 oz), divided
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups sweetened shredded coconut
  • 2 cups panko
  • 1 pound peeled and deveinied tail-on raw medium shrimp
  • vegetable oil
  • mango-habanero aioli

Directions

    Step 1:
  1. Combine honey, sugar, lemon zest and juice, and 1/2 tsp black pepper in a medium saucepan
  2. Bring to a simmer over medium-high, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves
  3. Remove from heat, and set aside

    4. Step 2:
  4. Stir together 2 cups flour, salt, and remaining 1/2 tsp black pepper in a shallow bowl until thoroughly combined
  5. Whisk together eggs, 1 cup water, baking powder, and remaining 1 cup flour in a separate bowl until just combined
  6. Stir together coconut and panko in a third bowl

    7. Step 3:
  7. Dredge 1 shrimp in salted flour mixture; shake off excess
  8. Dip into egg batter; let excess drip off
  9. Dredge in coconut mixiture, pressing lightly to adhere
  10. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  11. Repeat process using remaining shrimp
  12. Freeze shrimp until firm, about 30 min

    13. Step 4:
  13. Pour oil to a depth of 3 inches into a Dutch oven; heat over high to 325°F
  14. Working in about 3 batches, fry shrimp in hot oil until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes per batch
  15. Transfer cooked shrimp to a plate lined with paper towels to drain
  16. Let cool slightly, about 2 minutes
  17. Drizzle with honey mixture
  18. Serve with mango-habanero aioli
